Happy Journey (2014 Marathi film)


****Starring******
Atul Kulkarni,Priya Bapat
****Directed by****
Sachin Kundalkar
Released August 8, 2014
Everest Entertainment

This is the story of brother and sister. Atul Kulkarni is playing character of Old brother staying away from Family Priya Bapat is playing Role of younger sister. It all happened once upon a time in a beautiful town. A man returned to his hometown. He was angry, hurt and lonely. He came all detached. Back home a journey awaited him The fantastic journey that would change his entire life. The journey that he would cherish more than all other journeys he had taken. And so awaited his charming young sister. Who would take him along this fantastic trip. Of love & charming places. In the HOME CARAVAN . That they both have built for themselves.
