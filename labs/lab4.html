Art Lovers


My Name is Aparna. I'm one of the millions of Art Lover and who likes to create something beautiful out of available things like papers, used moving boxes.

I like paper quilling,like to play ping pong and these days I have developed interests in long tennis. Most of free time I spent in making some nice things out of quilling like earrings, flowers for wall decoration, nice photo frames all made of drawing paper etc.

Art is so vast that whenever I try to do something every time I create something new out of it. Anybody who would start doing it would not restrict themselves to do only Paper Qilling. I used quilling in decorating other things made of paper along with decorating things made of quilling only. I would like to share my ideas and would love to meet people sharing same interests learn from others experiences and ideas.

Cheers to all Art Lovers,
Aparna.
